003

Clearsight

AR SPY GLASSES · THROUGH-WALL HOLOGRAM · REMOTE · CONCEPT

◌ IN PLANNING

The goal: look at a wall through the glasses and see a holographic reconstruction of the person on the other side. Sensing is split by distance — mmWave radar (near), WiFi CSI (far), RF scan (ambient) — fused into a body pose that's drawn as a hologram on the lens. A handheld remote switches the view between full hologram, skeleton, and an x-ray build-up scan.

L1 · mmWave RADARnear range — presence & motion L2 · WiFi SENSING (CSI)far range — through-wall silhouettes L3 · RF SCAN (SDR)ambient — devices & signals nearby
